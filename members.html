Band Members Biographies

Learn more about our talented band members:

JayD

JayD, whose real name is John Doe, is the creative genius behind our music. He's the band's lead guitarist and has been passionately strumming the strings since the age of 10. JayD's love for music began with classic rock and blues, which continues to influence the band's signature sound. With his incredible guitar skills and innovative compositions, JayD is the driving force that sets Voltage Band apart. When he's not on stage, JayD enjoys experimenting with new guitar effects and jamming with his fellow band members.

Jayce

Meet Jayce, the band's talented guitarist. Born Jane Smith, Jayce's musical journey began at an early age. With classical training and a deep appreciation for diverse musical genres, Jayce brings a unique blend of styles to Voltage Band's sound. When not shredding on the guitar, Jayce can often be found exploring other instruments, composing music, and collaborating with fellow musicians. Jayce's versatility and dedication contribute to the band's dynamic compositions.

Jonny

Get to know Jonny, Voltage Band's passionate drummer. His real name is Robert Johnson, but he's better known as Jonny in the music world. Jonny's love for percussion started at a young age, and he can play a variety of percussion instruments, adding depth and rhythm to the band's music. His energetic drumming style and stage presence have become a defining element of the band's live performances. Jonny's musical knowledge extends beyond drumming, making him an essential part of the band's creative process.

Willsy

Willsy, whose real name is Sarah Wilson, is Voltage Band's talented bassist. Beyond her remarkable bass skills, Willsy is also the band's primary lyricist. Her passion for writing and storytelling complements the band's music, crafting captivating songs that resonate with listeners. Whether she's grooving on the bass or penning heartfelt lyrics, Willsy's creativity and dedication enrich the band's sonic experience.

Brownie

Meet Brownie, the keyboard maestro of Voltage Band. Born Michael Brown, he holds a degree in music composition, which brings a deep understanding of music theory to the band's compositions. Brownie's keyboard skills add layers of melody and harmony to the band's music, contributing to their distinct sound. When he's not creating musical magic with the band, Brownie enjoys composing instrumental pieces and collaborating with local artists.

Emi

Emi, whose real name is Emily Davis, is the band's amazing vocalist. She's a seasoned performer, having graced the stage in over 50 concerts around the world. Emi's powerful and emotive voice is a defining feature of Voltage Band's sound. Her passion for connecting with the audience through music is palpable in every performance. Emi's vocal range and stage presence captivate audiences and bring Voltage Band's music to life.
